Connect to your game (one time)

Before authoring, point the editor at your game install. After this, Save to game writes files to the correct folders automatically — no more path prompts.

Your browser needs permission to access the folder again. Click Reconnect — you should not need to browse for the folder unless you moved the game.

Choose the folder that contains exhibition-paperdoll/ and your game HTML:

  • exhibition-paperdoll/
  • CourseOfTemptation-Exhibition-appearance-dev.html (modder copy)
  • or CourseOfTemptationtest.html (installed copy)
